# Day 6 Trend-Ratio Baseline Findings

This is one predeclared illustrative baseline. No parameter optimization or
parameter grid was used. All results are development-only, and the locked
period was not accessed.

## Descriptive findings

- SPY buy-and-hold cumulative return: 1.297425.
- Trend-ratio gross cumulative return: 0.261377.
- Trend-ratio net cumulative return: -0.041234.
- At the stated one-basis-point placeholder cost, costs materially changed the
  cumulative result under a descriptive one-percentage-point threshold. The
  gross-minus-net cumulative-return difference was 0.302611.
- Total turnover was 2743.000000; turnover appears economically relevant to interpreting the baseline because it generated cumulative placeholder costs.
- Eligible positions were mostly long: long
  44.28%, short 32.89% and
  neutral 22.84%.
- On cumulative return, the net baseline underperformed SPY buy-and-hold. This
  is a descriptive comparison and is not evidence of statistical superiority.

## Limitations

Transaction costs are a placeholder, not a complete execution model. This
single baseline does not establish alpha, robustness, statistical
significance or deployability. Parameter sensitivity and genuine
out-of-sample validation remain future work.
